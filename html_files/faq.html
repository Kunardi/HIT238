FAQ

Just tap on Add To Do Lists and it will bring to page where you need to add the routine manually then Confirm to agree with the task.

On the schedule page, hit the delete button on the recorded task then will show confirm to delete or else cancel the delete.

On the schedule page, hit the edit button on the recorded task then it will bring you to page where you able to edit the task.

On the schedule page, hit the incomplete button on the recorded task then it will switch to complete button and vice versa.

On the schedule page, hit the tick box on the recorded task then it will show that task as priority.

The alarm will set automatic on once the schedule has made.
